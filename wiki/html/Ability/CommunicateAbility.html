Market Communications

Here you will find the tools, guidelines and directives to help you when you need to communicate externally

Brand and Market Communications

Market Communications is responsible for the Brand & Market Communications strategy, tools, procedudures and frameworks across Ericsson. This includes production and management of global creative platforms/campaigns/templates and relevant toolboxes and instructions for local market adaptions (advertizing, promotions, toolboxes).

External Communications channels

Everything within External Communications related to Marketing Communications area is the responibility of this organization. The different external communication channels here are, Ericsson Internet (www. ericsson.com), Customer Magazines, Newsletters, promotion materials, ads, Customer Success Stories, invitations, brochures, presentations, films, etc.

Global Communications Plan & Budgets

Market Communications secures and maintains the development of the Global Communications Plan and Message & Content Plan together with key Group Functions/BU/MU stakeholders, in order to secure consistent communications and budget decisions across all channels.

Supplier/Agency Management

Market Communications is responsible for management of suppliers and agencies within the area, to support market communication process.

Sponsorship management

Market Communications is also responsible fir Sponsorship policies and guidelines, as well as driving the major Ericsson Sponsorship initiatives worldwide.
